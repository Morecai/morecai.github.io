About Me

Old dog learning new tricks! No, it's not to late to begin again. I'm a single dad starting over in life, while trying to inspire my boys and show them that it is never to late to go back to school. Also that homework is a necessary evil that pays off in the end. In time you will find I am very persistent and dislike personal failure, though I do it with as well as the next guy, maybe a little more flair. I started in coding trying to save a businesses website, during a failing economy. I had fun dissecting HTML code and looking behind the curtain for lack of better words. All of which has brought me here, to build a new life coding, lets see what we can build, rip apart, tweak and play with!
